**Reconnaissance/ Initial Access:**

In this phase, the red team operator attempts to collect information relevant to the assessment while keeping as low of a profile as possible. In order to successfully perform effective, largely passive reconnaissance, the red team members need to access a variety of data sources and have a means for organizing the collected information to maximize its usability for the assessment.
