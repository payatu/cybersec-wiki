# Reconnaissance

Reconnaissance is the most important step in any assessment. It involves mapping out the attack surface of the target. A red team assessment involves recon of digital infrastructure as well as recon of organisation and employees. The common test cases are mentioned below.

## Digital Infrastructure \[Digital\]

* Subdomain Enumeration \(Using Gobuster\)
* Directory Enumeration \(Using Gobuster\)
* Port Scanning \(Using Nmap\)
* WebArchive \(Using Website\)
* Email Address in Previous Data Breach \[HIBP\] \(Using HIBP Website\)
* Common Username Across Sites / Credential Reuse \(Using [https://github.com/D4Vinci/Cr3dOv3r](https://github.com/D4Vinci/Cr3dOv3r)\)
* Image Metadata from Personal Blogs \[MSO Version & Author Name\]
* Whois
* Government Records - MCA?
* Shodan

## Organisation and Employees \[Physical\]

* Dumpster Diving \(This part involves going through the waste of the target company to find confidential information or information which might be beneficial to us.\)
* Fake Survey \(A fake survey can be used to gather some information required initially to begin the attack.\)
* Sales Call \(A fake sales call can be set up in order to extract juicy information which can help in the other phases\)
* Fake Meeting \(If attempts of psychical infiltration fail then an attempt can be made to setup a meeting with a fake pretext\)
* Maps \(Use the satellite view and Street view to have an idea of \)
* Employee List Generation from Linkedin & Facebook.
* Telephone Numbers \(From Search Engines and Company Pages\)
